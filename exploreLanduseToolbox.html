Landuse Toolbox Data Exploration

This R Markdown aims at transparently exploring options to calculate crop-specific physical areas from Landuse Toolbox data.

Option (a)

Percentage of violations

The percentage of cells where the sum of irrigated sugar cane and irrigated oilpalm exceeds irrigated physical land is

library(mrland)
library(scales)
percent <- label_percent(accuracy = 0.001)


harvestedArea <- readSource("LanduseToolbox", "harvestedArea")
physicalArea <- readSource("LanduseToolbox", "physicalArea")

diff_I <- physicalArea[ , , "irrigated"] -
          harvestedArea[ ,  , "irrigated.sugr_cane" ] - 
          harvestedArea[ ,  , "irrigated.oilpalm" ]

percent(mean( diff_I <0 ) )
## [1] "0.073%"

The percentage of cells where the sum of rainfed sugar cane and rainfed oilpalm exceeds rainfed physical land is

diff_R <- physicalArea[ , , "rainfed"] -
          harvestedArea[ ,  , "rainfed.sugr_cane" ] - 
          harvestedArea[ ,  , "rainfed.oilpalm" ]

percent(mean( diff_R <0 ) )
## [1] "0.115%"

On average we have

percent( (mean( diff_I <0 ) + mean( diff_R <0 ))/2 )
## [1] "0.094%"

violations.

Option (b)

The percentage of cells where the sum of total sugar cane and total oilpalm exceeds total physical land is

diff <- physicalArea[ , , "rainfed"] +
        physicalArea[ , , "irrigated"] -

        harvestedArea[ , , "rainfed.oilpalm" ] - 
        harvestedArea[ ,  , "irrigated.oilpalm" ]
